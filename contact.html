FAQ

Common Questions

Where are your offices located? ▼

Our global headquarters is in Singapore, with regional hubs in London (EMEA), New York (Americas), Mumbai (South Asia), and Dubai (MENA).

How long does compliance onboarding take? ▼

For standard businesses, our AI-powered KYB (Know Your Business) process completes in under 2 hours. For complex enterprise structures or regulated entities, manual review may take 1-3 business days.

Do you offer a sandbox environment? ▼

Yes, all developers get immediate access to our fully-featured sandbox environment upon signing up for a free account. No credit card or KYB is required for sandbox access.

What is your platform uptime SLA? ▼

We guarantee 99.99% uptime for Enterprise clients, backed by financial penalties. In 2024, our core payment routing API maintained 100% uptime.
