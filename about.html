Democratizing Access to Global Finance

We're building the infrastructure that connects the world's money. Stackly empowers businesses of all sizes to move value seamlessly, instantly, and securely across borders.

Our Mission

Breaking Down Financial Borders

Founded in 2020, Stackly was born from a simple frustration: moving money globally was slow, expensive, and opaque. We believed businesses deserved better infrastructure.

Today, our unified financial OS processes over $50B annually for 2 million users. We've replaced legacy banking rails with intelligent, API-first architecture that settles transactions in seconds instead of days.

Our Journey

The Stackly Story

From a small seed of an idea to a global financial platform.

2020

The Foundation

Stackly is founded in Singapore with a vision to rebuild cross-border payments. The first version of the platform launches, supporting USD and SGD.

2022

Series A & Global Expansion

Secured $45M Series A funding. Expanded operations to India, UK, and the US. Reached 100,000 active business users and launched Open Banking APIs.

2024

Stackly AI & Institutional Grade

Launched proprietary AI models for fraud detection and cash flow forecasting. Processed $20B in volume and obtained key regulatory licenses in EU and UAE.

2025

The Unified Financial OS

Crossed 2M users globally. Unveiled the Unified Financial OS integrating banking, lending, investments, and treasury. Achieved carbon-neutral operations.
