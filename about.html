About Stackly

We're building the financial infrastructure that empowers the next generation of wealth creators.

Our Mission

Democratizing Institutional Finance

Founded in 2020, Stackly was born from a simple observation: the world's best financial tools were locked behind closed doors, accessible only to hedge funds and ultra-high-net-worth individuals.

We set out to change that. Today, Stackly delivers institutional-grade algorithmic yield, quantum-resistant security, and precision analytics to over 2.4 million investors across 150 countries — all through an elegantly simple interface.
